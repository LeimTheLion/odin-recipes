Coconut pralines

Description

An exquisite dessert to end your meal with.

Ingredients

  • Cocoa powder
  • Ground coconut
  • Sugar
  • Soy ricotta

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients until you get a dough.
  2. Separate the dough into small balls.
  3. Cover the balls in more ground coconut.
  4. Put them in the fridge and serve them cold.
  5. Enjoy your dessert!
