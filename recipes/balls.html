Eggplant balls

Description

A tasty recipe which is also ideal for a vegan meal.

Ingredients

  • Eggplants
  • Ground bread
  • Ground almonds
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Ground spices

Steps

  1. Chop the eggplants, boil them and finally squash them.
  2. Mix the ingredients and then make small balls of dough.
  3. Fry the balls in a pan.
  4. Enjoy your meal!
