Spaghetti with walnuts

Description

A simple, delicious and healty dish which is also very easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Walnuts
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Arugula

Steps

  1. Pour a spoonful of salt into a pot of water and make it boil.
  2. Add the spaghetti to the boiling water and stir them until they are ready.
  3. Drain them and add walnuts, extra virgin olive oil and arugula to your dish.
  4. Enjoy your meal!
